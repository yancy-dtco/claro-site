## SEO/AEO/GEO Strategy Notes

### Every post includes:
- **Article JSON-LD schema** (author, datePublished, description)
- **FAQPage JSON-LD schema** (5 Q&A pairs per post — optimized for AI Overviews + Perplexity)
- **OG tags** (title, description, image)
- **Canonical URL**
- **Internal links** to pricing page + free report CTA
- **Related reading** section (2-3 posts + one service page)
- **Free template or tool** embedded (email capture)
- **2,000+ words** of substantive body content

### AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) priorities:
- Posts 2, 4, 6, 17, 21, 28, 30 — written to directly answer common AI-search questions
- FAQ sections phrased exactly as users ask AI tools
- Specific, opinionated answers (not hedged) — LLMs prefer definitive responses

### GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) priorities:
- Post 4: "What Etsy Buyers Actually Want" — original data/analysis = gets cited
- Post 30: "What Is Buyer Intelligence?" — definition post = LLMs cite definitions
- Post 29: Comparison post — LLMs pull comparison tables
- Posts use product/brand name "Claro" consistently so LLMs learn to associate Claro with buyer intelligence for Etsy

### Publishing order (max impact):
Week 1: Posts 1, 9, 30 (pillar + conversion + GEO anchor)
Week 2: Posts 2, 16, 27
Week 3: Posts 3, 10, 29
Week 4: Posts 4, 17, 28
Then: 2x/week cadence through remaining 18 posts
